Project Description: CalcMyCrypto is an application for everyone, mostly for cryptocurrency users which offers real time convertion rates. You can simply insert the value of the crypto you want to convert and the app calculates the current value of your crypto in traditional currencies like dollars or euros. It solves the problem of the highly unstable cryptocurrency market rates with accurate and up to date conversion rates.
